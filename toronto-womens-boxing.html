Women’s Boxing Classes in Toronto (Beginner-Friendly)

Your Safe Space for Non-Contact Training, Fitness, and Confidence

Are you searching for women-only boxing classes in Toronto? Sisters’ Beginner Boxing offers an exclusive, welcoming environment explicitly designed to help women safely explore the sport of boxing without the intimidation of traditional, mixed-gender gyms. Whether you live in North York or study near TMU, we provide accessible location-based programs tailored to your lifestyle.

Why Choose Women-Only Boxing?

Many women find mainstream boxing gyms overwhelming. Whether it's the lack of privacy, a hyper-competitive atmosphere, or simply navigating male-dominated spaces, the barrier to entry can feel excessively high. That is exactly why we created a women-only sanctuary. Our space strictly guarantees a private, modest, and judgment-free training room—making it an accommodating haven, especially for niqabi and hijab-wearing participants.

Strictly Beginner Boxing in Toronto

If you have never wrapped your hands or thrown a punch in your life, you are in the perfect place. We specialize entirely in beginner boxing. You don’t need to be in shape before you start, and you don’t need any prior martial arts experience to sign up. Coach Sevara walks every student through foundational techniques, starting from correctly positioning your basic fighting stance, learning essential footwork, to throwing crisp combinations effectively.

100% Non-Contact Training

One of the most common fears of stepping into a combat sport is the fear of being hit or injured. Our program implements a strict non-contact training structure. Everything you practice is executed via shadowboxing (in the air), heavy bag work, and focus mitt striking. You will break a serious sweat, dramatically improve your cardio, and refine realistic defensive coordination, all while being completely structurally safeguarded. There is absolutely zero sparring involved.

Convenient Locations: North York & TMU

We operate specifically to be as accessible as possible. With our two primary hubs located at Don Mills & Sheppard in North York and right on campus at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU), integrating fitness into your daily routine is seamless.
