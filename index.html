Meet the Coach

Coach Sevara

With an extensive background in martial arts and years of teaching experience, Coach Sevara is dedicated to creating a welcoming and completely supportive environment for women.

  • Taekwondo Black Belt & 2 years training in Boxing/Muay Thai

What to Expect

Expect a fun, welcoming environment where you can move, learn, and grow at your own pace.

You’ll learn real boxing fundamentals — footwork, punches, and movement — without any pressure or contact.

Every session is designed to help you feel stronger, more confident, and more comfortable in your body.

Come for the workout, stay for the energy, the progress, and the community.
